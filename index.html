About Me

I’ve been a professional voice artist for over 20 years now. My journey began when I was just 8 years old, recording for All India Radio. At 13, I got the opportunity to voice Doraemon in Hindi, which became one of the most iconic roles of my career. Since then, I’ve voiced a wide range of characters across cartoons, films, TV shows, and digital platforms—like Chhota Bheem, Pikachu, Bubbles from Powerpuff Girls, and even Arya Stark from Game of Thrones.

Over the years, I’ve worked with major studios and networks, dubbing in Hindi and other Indian languages. I also started my YouTube channel The Motor Mouth, where I share voice challenges, behind-the-scenes moments, and connect with fans. Voice acting has given me a creative space to experiment, grow, and bring life to characters that kids and adults love. It's been a fulfilling and exciting experience every step of the way.
